Come Stay With Us

We have many amenities for you to come and enjoy at Savannah’s Meadow. Our beautiful treehouses are a sight to behold. Once you are totally convinced you want to spend the rest of your life in one, make a reservation to stay a night.

By day you can bring a picnic, book a field trip, or come wander the gardens. Visit with Simon the donkey, the gander of geese, fish their ponds, or see the chickens grow. Come and visit our store to buy your fill of lavender essentials and Savannah Meadow specialities.
